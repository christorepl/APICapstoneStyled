Do COVID-19 infection rates have a correlation with poverty rates?

Although poverty is not the only factor in determining someone's likelihood to catch COVID-19, it has been shown to be a major factor in addition to race.

This app allows users to select one or more U.S. states and will display information about the poverty rates and COVID-19 rates of the given state or states. Numbers are meant to be an approximation.
